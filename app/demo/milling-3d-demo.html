Watch for

1
Machine powers up at home
G53 G0 Z0 retracts Z to the top of travel. G0 A0 B0 parks the rotary axes. DRO shows X=0 Y=0 Z=0 A=0 B=0.
2
G20 G90 — imperial, absolute
Program opens with G20 (inch) G90 (absolute) G94 (per-minute feed) G17 (XY plane). The standard Pentamachine V2 dialect.
3
Tool 4 loads, spindle 8500 RPM
M5 stop → M0 pause → T4 M6 tool change → G43 length compensation on → S8500 M3 starts the spindle CW at 8500 RPM. Feed set to 20 ipm.
4
A-axis tilts to 90°
G0 A90 rotates the trunnion so the B-platter is vertical, facing the spindle. Watch the trunnion body swing around its horizontal axis.
5
Square face pass at (1, 1) → (-1, -1) → (1, 1)
Four G1 moves cut a 2"×2" square perimeter on the face of the stock. Feed moves draw in Penta teal.
6
DRO ticks live every frame
X/Y/Z/A/B readout on the right panel — same readout you'll see on a real machine over Rockhopper WebSocket when the V2-10 is plugged in.
7
G49 + G53 Z0 safe retract
G49 cancels tool-length offset. G53 G0 Z0 moves Z to machine-coordinate zero at rapid. M5 stops spindle. M30 ends the program.
