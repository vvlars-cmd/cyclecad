cycleWASH Circular Engineering

Born from Circular Engineering

cycleWASH builds industrial bicycle washing machines — real hardware that keeps bikes in circulation longer. The DUO machine has 473 precision-engineered parts across 6 sub-assemblies, designed in Autodesk Inventor and built in Germany.

Every part is designed for disassembly, repair, and reuse. That's circular engineering — designing products that fight the throwaway economy from the first sketch.

cycleCAD Democratizing Design

The Tool We Needed — Now Free for Everyone

Designing the DUO machine meant fighting $4,000/year CAD licenses, proprietary file formats, and tools that lock you into one vendor's ecosystem. We built cycleCAD to solve our own problem — and then open-sourced it.

Browser-based, zero-install, MIT-licensed. The same parametric modeling power that designed a production machine, now accessible to anyone with a web browser. That's the democratization of CAD.

Machines Creating Machines

Here's the endgame: if AI can do CAD, then a machine can design its own replacement parts. Its own upgrades. Its own next generation.

A cycleWASH machine scans its worn brush assembly. The AI copilot in cycleCAD analyzes the geometry, identifies the wear pattern, redesigns the part for longer life, generates the G-code, and sends it to the 3D printer on the factory floor. No human touched a mouse.

That's the true cycle. Machine → AI understands the machine → AI improves the design → better machine. Circular engineering at the speed of thought.

Humans tell what they want. Machines produce the parts, assemble them, and deliver the final product. No drawings. No factories full of people interpreting blueprints. Just intent in, product out. cycleCAD is the bridge between human imagination and autonomous manufacturing.
