Executive Summary

Fusion 360 has approximately 210 distinct features across 21 product areas. cycleCAD currently implements 95 features (45% coverage). Of those, 28 are partial/stub implementations. The largest gaps are in Simulation, CAM, Generative Design, Drawing/Documentation, and Surface Modeling — which together account for 60+ missing features.
