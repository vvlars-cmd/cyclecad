OUR STORY

One Prefix. One Philosophy.

The "cycle" in both brands isn't coincidence — it's the DNA of everything we build. Circular thinking applied to machines, and now to the tools that design them.

cycleWASH
Circular Engineering

cycleWASH builds industrial bicycle washing machines — real hardware that keeps bikes in circulation longer. Complex machines with hundreds of precision-engineered parts across multiple sub-assemblies, designed in Autodesk Inventor and built in Germany.

Every part is designed for disassembly, repair, and reuse. That's circular engineering — designing products that fight the throwaway economy from the first sketch.

cycleCAD
Democratizing Design

Designing complex machines meant fighting $4,000/year CAD licenses, proprietary file formats, and tools that lock you into one vendor's ecosystem. We built cycleCAD to solve our own problem — and to power the circular economy.

Browser-based, zero-install, AI-agent-ready. Built so AI agents can design autonomously, unskilled workers can build with confidence, and anyone with a browser can participate in the circular economy.

THE PROBLEM

CAD software is stuck in the past

Requires screen, mouse, keyboard

Traditional CAD demands a trained human sitting at a workstation. An AI agent can't use SolidWorks. An unskilled worker can't use Inventor. The interface IS the bottleneck.

$680 — $6,000 per year

Professional CAD tools cost thousands per seat per year. Small teams, field workers, and developing nations are priced out of designing their own products.

No intelligence built in

Traditional CAD is a dumb drawing tool. No AI analysis, no maintenance planning, no autonomous design. Every action requires a trained human operator.

Designed for the throwaway economy

No tool helps you design for disassembly, repair, or reuse. No wear prediction, no maintenance scheduling, no circular economy thinking. Design once, throw away, repeat.

THE SOLUTION

Browser-native CAD

Full parametric 3D modeler running entirely in your browser. Three.js-powered viewport with real-time rendering. No plugins, no install, no waiting.
