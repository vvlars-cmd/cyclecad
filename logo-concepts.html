cycleCAD Logo Concepts

Gold + Blue color scheme — The OS of Things

Concept 1: Hexagon Cycle

Engineering hexagon (precision, nuts & bolts) with gold circular arrows (the cycle). Blue = technology. Gold = value creation.

Concept 2: C² Monogram

Two interlocking C's — one blue (cycle), one gold (CAD). Crosshair in center = precision engineering. The arrow = continuous cycle.

Concept 3: Gear + Infinity

Gear = mechanical engineering. Gold infinity loop inside = the never-ending cycle. Machines creating machines, forever.

Concept 4: Split Wordmark

"cycle" in blue, "CAD" in gold. Clean, bold, reads instantly. Small gold cycle arrow bridges the two words. Works at any size.

Concept 5: Delta Cycle

Triangle = creation, building upward. Rotating gold ring = the cycle. Inner delta = change, iteration. Dynamic and forward-looking.

Recommended: Icon + Wordmark (Light)

Works on light backgrounds. Gold cycle arrows in blue circle = brand mark. Pairs with split wordmark. Versatile across print, web, and slides.
