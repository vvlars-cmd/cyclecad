📊 5 Ways Models Generate Revenue
Every model you share is an asset with multiple income streams.
🛒

1. Direct Downloads

Users pay tokens to download your STEP/GLB/STL files. You set the price. You earn 70% of every sale. Popular models generate passive income for years.

🤖

2. Agent Usage Fees

When AI agents reference your model as a component in someone else's design, you earn micro-royalties per use. An M8 bracket used 10,000 times = 10,000 payouts.

🧬

3. Derivative Royalties

Someone forks your gearbox and modifies it? The original creator earns 15% of derivative sales. Your work compounds across the entire ecosystem.

📐

4. Template Licensing

Parametric models used as templates earn tokens every time someone generates a variant. A configurable bracket template = infinite variations, infinite earnings.

🏢

5. Enterprise Procurement

Companies buy bulk token packages for their teams. When engineers use marketplace models in production, creators earn enterprise-grade payouts.

3D Print Pipeline

AI-powered slicing — auto-orient for strength, generate optimal supports, estimate print time & material cost. Supports FDM, SLA, SLS, and MJF. Export G-code for Prusa, Bambu, Creality, or send directly to cloud print services. 20 🪙 per slice job.

⚡

Laser Cutting Pipeline

Sheet metal to DXF — auto-unfold 3D sheet metal parts, nest multiple parts on a sheet for minimum waste, calculate kerf compensation, generate power/speed/frequency tables for steel, aluminum, acrylic. 15 🪙 per nested sheet.

🔩

CNC Toolpath Pipeline

3-axis to 5-axis — roughing, finishing, adaptive clearing, contour, pocket, drilling, thread milling. AI selects optimal cutting strategies, calculates feeds/speeds per material. Post-processors for Fanuc, Haas, Siemens, Heidenhain. 50 🪙 per toolpath set.

🔄

CNC Lathe / Turn-Mill

Rotational parts — facing, OD/ID turning, grooving, threading, parting. Live tooling for turn-mill centers. AI detects rotational symmetry and auto-generates lathe ops. 40 🪙 per program.

Don't know CNC programming? Your agent does. Tell it: "Generate 3-axis toolpaths for aluminum, Haas VF-2, 0.05mm tolerance" and it selects tools, calculates feeds/speeds, generates collision-free paths. AI agents make CAM accessible to everyone.

Toolpath Marketplace
